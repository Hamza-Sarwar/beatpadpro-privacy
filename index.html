Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Summary: Beat Pad Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Introduction

Beat Pad Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for the Beat Pad Pro mobile application.

Data Collection

Beat Pad Pro does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements

Permissions

Beat Pad Pro requests the following permission:

  • Vibration (VIBRATE): Used solely to provide haptic feedback when you tap the beat pads. This permission does not access any personal data.

Data Storage

All app data, including your recorded loops and theme preferences, is stored locally on your device. This data is:

  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

Internet Access

Beat Pad Pro does not require or use internet access. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Beat Pad Pro is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Beat Pad Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
